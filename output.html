A small predictable markup language.

> To the big site while turning over a page. > > 한 쪽씩 넘기며 한(큰) 쪽으로.

Goals:

- Make the language predictable - Have a small ruleset - Make documents store contents instead of styles - Make components creation and adoption easy - Make documents platform-agnostic - Make tool integration easy and rich

Not a Goals:

- Complicated powerful typesetting system (like LaTeX) : We aren't making a paper book. - Concise stackable emphasize syntax (like Markdown) : We wish to write more instead decorate.

Remaining Questions:

- How to integrate a complex syntax like a table or a sublist? -> Thinking of indentation and dedentation syntax, and a syntax just for the table.
